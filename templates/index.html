About Cyclique

Cyclique is a modern, AI-powered period tracking application designed to help women understand and manage their menstrual health. We combine cutting-edge machine learning technology with an intuitive, beautiful interface to provide accurate predictions and valuable insights.

Our mission is to empower women with knowledge about their bodies. By tracking your cycle, symptoms, and mood patterns, you can better understand your health and make informed decisions about your wellness.

Built with privacy and security in mind, Cyclique ensures your personal health data remains confidential. We use advanced LSTM neural networks trained on real cycle data to provide personalized predictions that improve over time.

Whether you're trying to conceive, managing period symptoms, or simply want to stay in tune with your body, Cyclique is here to support your journey to better health and wellness.
